Burger

Juicy grilled burger

Half a pound of eighty twenty ground beef, shaped into a patty placed with lots of toppings and condiments into a toasted buns.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • ketchup
  • cheese
  • 80/20 ground beef
  • buns

Steps

  1. form the patty
  2. cut the toppings
  3. grill the bacon
  4. grill the patty
  5. cut and toast the bun
  6. put all ingredients in the bun
