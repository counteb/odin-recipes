chocolate chip cookies

Warm gooey chewy cookies with milk chocolate chips. They smell better than they look, but not as good as they taste.

Ingredients

  • flower
  • salt
  • sugar
  • butter
  • chocolate chips
  • eggs

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. melt the butter
  3. mix the dry ingredients
  4. add the wet ingredients
  5. form into small balls
  6. place on a tray
  7. bake for fifteen minutes
