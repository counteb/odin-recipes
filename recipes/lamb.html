Lamb

Tender flavorfull lamb shank

A lamb shank that has cooked slow and low for five hours. Developing complex flavors and a tender texture that melts anywhere you put it.

Ingredients

  • lamb shank
  • salt
  • pepper
  • parsley
  • corn starch
  • water

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 240
  2. salt and pepper the shank
  3. place it in a duch oven
  4. add half a cup of water
  5. put in oven for five hours
  6. chop up parsley and sprinkle on top
