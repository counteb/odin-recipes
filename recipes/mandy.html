Spiced chicken legs and rice

An incredible perfectly balanced mix of spices, that permeate both the meat and rice. Usually taking a full day to prepare, this is for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • basmati rice
  • saffron
  • chicken stock
  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • spices

Steps

  1. soak saffron in boiling water
  2. rinse rice
  3. chop onion
  4. great ginger and garlic
  5. cook oil and spices in a pot
  6. add gallic ginger and onions
  7. put in chicken and chicken stock
  8. cook till tender
  9. remove chicken and set aside
  10. add rice to broth
  11. cook 15 minutes
  12. put saffron on the chicken
  13. put chicken on the rice
